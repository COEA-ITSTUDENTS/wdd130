History

Zarahemla White Water Rafting, located in Abuja, Nigeria, brings the thrill of river adventure to the heart of West Africa. Founded with a vision to combine outdoor recreation with safe, family-friendly adventure, Zarahemla began as a small initiative to introduce white water rafting to the Nigerian capital's scenic rivers. Inspired by the global growth of rafting in the 20th century and the natural beauty surrounding Abuja, Zarahemla launched guided rafting tours that highlight the region’s unique landscape and waterways. Over the years, it has grown into a premier destination for adventure tourism in Nigeria, attracting both locals and international visitors. With a strong focus on safety, environmental responsibility, and unforgettable experiences, Zarahemla White Water Rafting has become a pioneer in outdoor sports in Nigeria—offering a refreshing way to explore nature while promoting eco-tourism and healthy living in Abuja and beyond.
